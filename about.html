About Dr. Siamak Darehbaghi

Dr. Siamak Darehbaghi earned his medical degree from Midwestern University in Arizona and completed residency training in Psychiatry at LewisGale Medical Center in affiliation with Virginia Tech and the Veterans Affairs Health System.

He is board certified in Psychiatry and licensed in New York State.

He serves as an Associate Professor of Clinical Psychiatry at Albert Einstein School of Medicine, where he teaches, mentors, and engages in academic leadership.

His approach is warm, collaborative, and evidence-based. He focuses on helping adults navigate anxiety, depression, mood disorders, ADHD, trauma, psychotic disorders, and life transitions.
